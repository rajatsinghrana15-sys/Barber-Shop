How to Book

1. How to book an appointment?

  1. Select Service: Click on the "Book Appointment" button.
  2. Choose Date & Time: Select your preferred date and time from the calendar.
  3. Enter Details: Fill in your name, phone number, and email address.
  4. Confirm: Click "Schedule Event." Your appointment is now booked!

2. How to Reschedule or Cancel?

If you need to change your time or cancel the booking, follow these steps:

  • Check your Email: Open the confirmation email you received after booking.
  • Use the Buttons: You will find "Reschedule" and "Cancel" buttons inside that email.
  • Make Changes: Simply click the button to select a new time or cancel your booking instantly.

If you face any issues, please feel free to call or WhatsApp us!
