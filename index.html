How to Manage Your Appointment

1. How to Book a New Appointment

  1. Choose Service: Click the "Book Appointment" button.
  2. Select Time: Choose your preferred date and time from the calendar.
  3. Fill Details: Enter your name, phone number, and email.
  4. Submit: Click the "Schedule Event" button. You will receive a confirmation email immediately.

2. How to Reschedule or Cancel

If your plans change, you can easily manage your booking:

  • Find your Email: Open the confirmation email you received when you booked.
  • Access Options: Scroll down to find the "Reschedule" or "Cancel" buttons.
  • Update: Click the button to select a new time or cancel your appointment entirely.
